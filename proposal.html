An information technology problem that a fitness app that provides a workout plan, diet, and rest guidance could face is a lack of personalization. Many fitness apps offer generic workout plans and diets that may not consider an individual's unique fitness goals, physical limitations, or dietary restrictions. Additionally, the app may not be able to adjust the workout plan or diet as the user progresses and reaches new fitness milestones. This issue could lead to frustration for users who are not seeing the desired results and may cause them to stop using the app altogether. Another issue could be the lack of integration with other health-related apps and devices, such as heart rate monitors, sleep trackers, and nutrition apps, which would provide more comprehensive data and personalized recommendations to the user.

One previous or existing attempt to solve the problem of lack of personalization in fitness apps is using machine learning and artificial intelligence. The app can adjust the workout plan and diet recommendations by using data from the user's activity level, fitness goals, and progress. Additionally, by integrating with other health-related apps and devices, the app can gather more comprehensive data and provide even more personalized recommendations to the user. Some existing fitness apps such as MyFitnessPal, Fitbit, and Endomondo already use this technology to personalize workout and diet plans for their users.

Our Vision

A common area of interest in our group is fitness, and we are looking to build a web app that contains various different strength training excercises . Our webapp aims to provide three functionalities, filter, search, description.

Our app will allow the user to filter the various exercises by the muscle group it targets as well as the equipment they have access to. For example, a user might select chest and shoulder exercises, and the barbell as equipment they have access to. Our app will then recommend exercises such as the bench press and the military press to the user.

Another function we wish to provide a information page for each different excercise. It will include instructions on how to perform the exercises as well as picture demonstrations. It will also include various other information relevant to the exercise such as rep range, optimal techniques. We also want to implement a comment section for each excercise so users could leave sugguestions and tips for each excercise.

We also wish to implement a search bar for users who simply wish to look up the information pages for each exercise as a quick reference on how to perform them.
